About Us

Healing with Heart, Honoring a Vision

At Kartik Oraon Multi-Speciality Hospital, we’re more than just a healthcare facility; we are a beacon of hope and support for marginalized communities. Founded in honor of Baba Kartik Oraon, a visionary leader and advocate for the Adivasi people, our hospital embodies his commitment to social justice, empowerment, and access to quality healthcare for all.

Empowering Underserved Communities

Our mission is to bring high-quality healthcare to marginalized communities, uplifting lives through accessible and affordable medical services.

Inspired by Kartik Oraon's Vision

Guided by Baba Kartik Oraon's legacy, we are committed to social justice, community well-being, and a healthier future for all.

Our Services

Excellence in Healthcare Across Multiple Specialties

In keeping with Baba Kartik Oraon’s vision, the hospital is committed to accessible healthcare for all, with a special focus on underserved regions. His dedication to integrating education, healthcare, and community welfare is at the heart of everything we do.
